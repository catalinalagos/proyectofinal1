Catálogo Colectivo

2020

Resumen

Catálogo colectivo is an app designed for students, that helps you choose your classes and organize each semester, based on the experiences of classmates from your university, so that they can have an optimal academic load. Students can voice their opinions of the classes they have already taken, and look for references of the ones they're interested in taking. The design focus was set on usability, so it is simple and useful, but also playful.

Going to college was something like those competitions we used to see on television where you blindly had to choose a door. Being terribly curious, I needed to see what was behind each one, so I took several unnecessary turns, but thanks to this I found the world of design and gave myself permission to explore it.

Each academic unit gives you an idea of which classes you should take each semester. For me this was rather a suggestion, and it took me several semesters to improve my poor class-matching skills. I underestimated what a well-organized semester could do for me, my performance, and my well-being.
